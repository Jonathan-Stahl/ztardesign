Video Games Console Collection

The design brief for this project involves developing a website using HTML and CSS, based on a topic selected from the Powerhouse Museum Collection sets. I chose the video games collection, expanding outside the collection to focus on the iconic Nintendo and Sega consoles. This approach allowed me to create a website with more relevant and captivating content, providing greater inspiration for the design process.
