WIL - Canberra Modern

In collaboration with the University of Canberra and Canberra Modern, a brief was provided to propose a redesign of the existing Canberra Modern website. The brief emphasized adherence to brand guidelines, reflecting Canberra's modernist identity, and ensuring a fully responsive design. In response, the website was initially prototyped in Figma and subsequently developed using HTML and CSS.

It features:

  • Mobile-Responsive Layouts: Adaptable designs for various devices.
  • Unique Aesthetic: Powerful visual message that showcases Canberra's Modernism.
  • Hero Content: Bold and visually engaging focal points.
  • Simplistic Footers: Clean layouts with social media links.
  • Page Flow: Thoughtfully structured content presentation.
