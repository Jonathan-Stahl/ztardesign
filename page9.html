Synthic: An Original Typeface

Synthic is a that is set apart and versatile. It has been adapted to be functional for use as both a body copy and display font. Synthic’s notable design uses geometrical shape sits in every letter, symbol and number, mostly done through mathematical and visual measurements to maintain its perfection. The typeface is tidy and consistent all round, being aesthetically pleasing to look at and comfortable to read. It excels at being a modern and futuristic typeface that also involves some traditional typeface design aspects like the weight contrasts in Times New Roman. Overall, Synthic is a unique, future inspired and compelling type-face.
