Sound Design Projects

Short Soundscape

The results from an interview between the subject reveals writing as something they are passionate about. This audio short story takes snippets from the full 5 minute interview and brings it together into a seamless monologue. Foley was added to increase the message of the monologue, also taking account of background and incidental sounds used, music and effects, editing, and narrative arc and structure. At last, an audio narrative featuring a seamless monologue about one’s passion in writing was produced.

Movie Scene Soundtrack

A soundtrack recreation of a scene from Arcane S1 E6 When These Walls Come Tumbling Down. It is written by Alex Yee and composed by myself. The voice actors for this scene are Saskia Day playing as Jinx, Gloria Stahl playing as Vi, and Sana Chatley playing as Caitlyn. The scene focuses on two sisters, Jinx and Vi, reuniting after years of separation, enhancing the emotional state the characters feel through the development of the composition. The original scene can be watched here.
